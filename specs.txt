===
What should this tool do?
===

1. Create new projects
2. Add items to existing projects
3. Categorize items by due date, priority, topic, timestamp, etc.
4. Complete items
5. Allow different kinds of items
    - Tasks (-t): To-do items with deadlines, priority, etc.
    - Logs (-l): Records of what has been completed today
    - Notes (-n): Nuggets of useful information


===
Example use cases
===

---
Example 1
---
Create a new project.
--- 

pom new myproject

Creating new project 'myproject'
Done!

---
Example 2
---
Add an item to existing project.
---

pom new myproject

pom add -t "Finish the design"

pom overdue
